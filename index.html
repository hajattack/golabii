Why Golabii

A new kind of drink
from a new kind of company

A new kind of attempt at fixing the world. We're tired of corporations and people in power not caring about humanity. We are a new startup selling premium, never-before-seen ingredients and cultural drinks into your life to brighten up your day and your soul. Our ancient recipe is steam-distilled, superfood, anti-inflammatory, and neurotrophic — and our goal is to raise money and awareness to help the people of Iran, especially the women, students, and all those hurt in the protest genocide.

our dream

Building Hope,
One Sip at a Time

We are currently in contact with schools, clubs, and community leaders of the diaspora and expats to help make this a long-term effort for Iran. (We have unfortunately lost contact with our team inside Iran.)
