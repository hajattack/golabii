What is Golabii

Golabii is a saffron-forward beverage blending rose hydrosol, cardamom and other botanicals. Balanced sweetness, bright acidity, and a long saffron finish make it both refreshing and indulgent.

  • Saffron, rose, cardamom and botanicals
  • Botanical hydrosols, no artificial flavors
  • Refreshing served chilled or mixable

Our mission

We are building a saffron‑forward beverage that supports communities in need. By celebrating Persian flavors and hospitality, we hope to create a ritual that gives back.

  • Support farmers and artisans through fair partnerships
  • Foster cultural connection and storytelling
  • Directly fund relief and rebuilding initiatives
