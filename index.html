The drink

Small-batch saffron beverage layered with rose hydrosol and cardamom. Balanced sweetness, bright acidity, and a long saffron finish.

  • Real saffron, micro-batched
  • Botanical hydrosols, no artificial flavors
  • Chilled ready-to-drink or mixable

Why Golabii

Inspired by Persian flavors and hospitality, we designed a luxurious, everyday ritual that funds real help. Golabii channels sales into transparent donations for Gaza, Iran, and Kurdistan initiatives.

Craft

From saffron sourcing to hydrosol balance, we document the build—the wins and the mistakes—so the community owns the process.

Design

Modular bottle concepts, upcyclable bricks, and a brand system that feels old-world and future-proof at the same time.

Impact

Donation receipts and monthly summaries. No hand-wavy charity math.
