01 — The Essence

Liquid Gold.

Golabii is a study in balance. We combine whole saffron threads, delicate rose petals, and green cardamom to create a beverage that feels like a memory of a Persian garden.

  • ✦ Whole saffron threads
  • ✦ Damask rose water
  • ✦ Cardamom essence
02 — The Mission

Rooted in
Hospitality.

Our mission is to honour the cultures that inspire our flavours. Golabii is not just a drink; it's a bridge between worlds, supporting the farmers and artisans who keep these traditions alive.

We pledge a portion of profits to community relief projects.

03 — The Vision

Growing
Slowly.

We believe in micro-batches and massive care. Our goal is to build a sustainable supply chain that values quality over speed, ensuring every bottle tells a story.
