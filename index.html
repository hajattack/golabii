What is it?

Learn about the ingredients that make Golabii special. We combine saffron, rose, cardamom and other botanicals to create a balanced, floral beverage with a bright finish.

  • Whole saffron threads and rose petals
  • Botanical hydrosols for natural flavour
  • Serve chilled on its own or mix into cocktails

Our mission

Our mission is to honour the cultures that inspire Golabii. By supporting farmers and artisans and donating profits, we celebrate hospitality and give back to the communities who shape our flavours.

  • Support growers and makers through fair partnerships
  • Share stories of culture and connection
  • Fund relief and community projects with our profits

Our goals

Our goals describe how we'll bring Golabii to life. We plan to grow responsibly, engage our supporters and build a sustainable supply chain rooted in community.

  • Craft micro‑batches with quality and care
  • Grow an engaged community of supporters
  • Collaborate with local farms and makers
