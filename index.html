Why Golabii

A new kind of drink
from a new kind of company

We believe everyone deserves the best no matter who they are. Taking care of each other has always been the best thing about being human. We are not in it for the money — that's how you know we don't skim on the premium ingredients for our people (you!). An ancient floral blend of steam-distilled drink of the kings, used for its neurotrophic, cognitive, and superfood anti-inflammatory health benefits. We're sharing our insanely refreshing secret recipe in hopes we can get attention to Iran, crowdsourcing awareness and funding for those fighting for freedom who have been through the worst massacre of modern history with 36,000 killed in 48 hours followed by 20 days of internet blackout to be silenced. We plan on using our net profits to invest, waiting for the moment Iran is free, to help heal the brave who bled for freedom.

our dream

Building Hope,
One Sip at a Time

We aspire to be a non-profit decentralized startup project, revolutionary in our approach to try something never done before. Every bottle sold supports those who stood up against tyranny and gave their lives for freedom. We are organizing the community to help the community. Our first focus is to help all those who gave their blood for freedom, from raising awareness and money to invest in the future of the younger generation of Iran, providing investment for rebuilding infrastructure and mental health resources for all those who suffered.
